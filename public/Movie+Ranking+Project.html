The Best Movies According to Ajay Chandra

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Hacksaw ridge

This movie teaches us about integrity.
The lead cast has done an incredible job portraying their roles
It taught me how we should stick to our beliefs during adversity

12th fail

This movie is a biopic of an Indian IPS officer.
The movie is as raw as it gets.
It shows all the struggles of an UPSC aspirant.

Ghazi

This movie is history.
It gave me those chills i crave in a theatrical experience.
